A beginner's website, often marked by simplicity and enthusiasm, is like a digital canvas where they showcase their newly acquired web development skills. It might feature a straightforward layout, essential information, and a glimpse of their portfolio. The design might be minimalistic, yet it demonstrates their eagerness to learn and create.
